Pegasus

ePortfolios give students the opportunity to organize and reflect on their work while making integrative connections between their varied curricular and extracurricular experiences. Despite our vision for their use, current eportfolio systems still focus on one aspect of the processâ€”collection, reflection, representation, or evaluation. Pegasus is an ePortfolio application currently under development that brings those eportfolio processes together in one tool, as well as expand on the possibilities of adaptive spaces for reflection.

  • Collect

    Upload documents, video clips, or whatever you can think of to your Library, which you can access from any computer, tablet, or mobile device!
  • Select

    Once you have items in your library, you can tag them, write reflections for them, group them into sets, and share them with anyone.
  • Present

    Build rich, beautiful representations of your collections with a simple drag-and-drop editor. Then share them with the world!
  • Assess

    Open your ePortfolios to feedback from peers, family, and your social networks! Find your professors' rubrics, and see how they graded you.
